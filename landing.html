Simple remedies for a healthier you:

Cough and Cold

Try this home remedy: Tulsi Tea

Tulsi (holy basil) tea is known for its soothing properties and can help relieve cough and cold symptoms.

Sore Throat

Consider this remedy: Honey and Warm Water

Mixing honey with warm water can provide relief for a sore throat. Make sure the water is not too hot.

Headache

Try this home remedy: Peppermint Oil

Apply diluted peppermint oil to your temples for a natural remedy to alleviate headache symptoms.

Indigestion

Consider this remedy: Ginger Tea

Ginger tea can help soothe indigestion. Drink a cup of warm ginger tea after meals for relief.
